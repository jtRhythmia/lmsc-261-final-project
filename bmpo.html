Berklee Motion Picture Orchestra

Contact Information: hpso@berklee.edu

The Berklee Motion Picture Orchestra (BMPO) is a cross-collegiate organization and the largest student-run orchestra at Berklee with a performing strength of 70 musicians. Every semester, BMPO holds a concert that centers around

film music arranged by students. By engaging in weekly rehearsals and delivering captivating public performances away from the Berklee campus, BMPO has performed soundtracks from Batman, Harry Potter, Interstellar, The

Matrix, Star Wars and many more. The BMPO is dedicated to cultivating musical artistry, foestering teamwork, and igniting a lifelong enthusiasm for film music within a communituy that celebrates diversity and inclusion. Our

mission extends beyond the confines of our institution, striving to enrich the cultural landscape of our college and the broader community through transformative musical experiences.
