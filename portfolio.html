Jalan Journey

Headquartered in Singapore, Jalan Journey is a pioneering developer, publisher, and promoter of digital games that inspire empathy and drive social impact. We design and deliver interactive learning experiences that focus on

financial literacy, inclusivity, and sustainability, reaching students, organizations, and individuals worldwide. Our products are utilized in schools, team-building workshops, and personal growth initiatives, available through

digital platforms and tailored packages. Since our inception, Jalan Journey has empowered over 5,000 students across 45 schools in 4 countries.


Thoughtfully:

A free, interactive tool designed to spark reflection and facilitate structured, meaningful, open-ended discussions in the classroom. Players join a session, respond to prompts, and share their reasoning.


Scam Sleuths:

A scenario-based game that educates players on cybercrime in this digital age. Players learn how to investigate suspicious messages, track online fraudsters, and identify phishing emails to protect virtual citizens from digital threats.


Wayfinders:

Navigate sensory challenges as a character on the autism spectrum. This game provides a glimpse into how individuals on the spectrum process information and why they may react in certain ways to everyday situations.

Players learn about sensory triggers and processing, including sound, light, smells, and tactile sensations, gaining a better understanding of invisible challenges individuals with autism face daily due to sensory overload.
