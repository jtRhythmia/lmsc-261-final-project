The Association of Southeast Asian Nations (ASEAN) Show is a celebration of Southeast Asian cultural heritages that highlight the rich traditions and unique musical identities of the nations represented at Berklee College of Music.

Our concert at the Berklee Performance Center on March 11, 2025 featured student compositions/arrangements, and talented musicians from Singapore, Malaysia, Indonesia, Philippines, Thailand, Vietnam, Cambodia, Myanmar and

Brunei, offering an unforgettable evening of diverse yet interconnected sounds and stories. This show was a historical moment at Berklee College of Music because it was the first-ever collaboration between established student-run

clubs from these countries. These clubs aim to introduce Berklee and the greater Boston community to a vibrant fusion of Southeast Asian traditions. By blending traditional and contemporary musical elements, the performance

delivered a one-of-a-kind, electrifying experience that showcased the dynamic spirit of the regions' rich cultural heritages.
