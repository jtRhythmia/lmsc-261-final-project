Audire Soundtrack Collective

Contact Information: audiresoundtrackchoir@gmail.com

Audire Soundtrack Collective is a student-run, performance-based ensemble at Berklee College of Music that strives to spread the joy of music from visual media - video games, television, film, anime and musicals. By featuring

student arrangements and compositions in the form of vocal and instrumental performances, Audire strives to provide ample opportunities for students to develop their professional musicianship and artistry in performing, writing,

conducting, audio and video engineering, marketing and leadership.
