Upside Down Universe

Step into a world where Sight, Sound, and Soul collide...

Contact Information: upsidedownuniverseinfo@gmail.com

Upside Down Universe reimagines artistic performance through innovation and collaboration. Combining original music composed by Berklee College of Music students, evocative visuals designed by Emerson College and

Massachusetts College of Art & Design (MassArt), and cutting-edge technology, this multimodal and multisensory production explores the interconnectedness of humanity and the environment.


Upside Down Universe transforms the Berklee Performance Center into a fully immersive environment by incorporating innovative layouts, projection surfaces and audience engagement tools. Sponsored by the Berklee Music

Business/Management Department and MF Dynamics, and parterned with CrowdLED and EPSON, this production offers an inspiring artistic experience and a platform for Boston's creative colleges to collaborate and enhance

the multidisciplinary nature of the show.
